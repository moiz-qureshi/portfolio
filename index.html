04 — Process

Six phases. One owner.

No handoff between strategy and execution

I build the plan and I run the plan. The person who scoped the risk is the same person remediating it, which means nothing gets lost in translation between a slide deck and a pull request.

01 — Due Diligence
Stand up C2 infrastructure and run real technical assessments against the target — before the deal closes and before the team knows they're being evaluated.
02 — Discovery
Post-announcement deep dive. Map every service, credential, cloud account, and dependency. Build the full picture of what we're inheriting.
03 — Remediation
Fix the things that would keep a CISO up at night. Critical vulns, broken auth, missing encryption, wide-open storage buckets — addressed in priority order.
04 — Onboarding
Wire the acquisition into enterprise IAM, EDR, CSPM, and secret management. By the end, every identity, endpoint, and cloud resource is visible to the parent org.
05 — Compliance
Keep SOC 2 running, schedule the mandatory pen test, close the audit gaps. Certifications don't lapse on my watch.
06 — Hand-Off
Deliver the Security Dossier — every risk documented, every remediation tracked, a clear roadmap for the BISO taking over. Clean handoff, no loose ends.
